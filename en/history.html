Our Journey

The Story of GROUND

A challenge that began with one person's dream to change the rules of logistics

GROUND's story began long before our founding in 2015. It started with founder Hiroyuki Miyata's experience across banking, consulting, and the front lines of logistics, combined with insights into the potential of logistics robotics he witnessed during his time at Rakuten. These elements came together to form GROUND Inc.

Over the past decade, GROUND has pursued genuine transformation in the logistics industry through partnerships, funding, and proprietary product development. From robot deployment to AI software development, to the birth of the logistics OS "GWES" — our journey is just beginning.

CHAPTER 0 — PREHISTORY
1996
1996 – 2004

From Finance and Consulting to Logistics

Founder Hiroyuki Miyata began his career at Sumitomo Banking Corporation (now Mitsubishi UFJ Bank). He later worked as a management consultant at Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu (now ABeam Consulting). In 2004, he joined ASKUL as Director of Logistics Centers, diving into the logistics industry. The challenges he witnessed at the ground level became the foundation for GROUND's future.

PREHISTORY
2007
2007 – 2014

Rakuten Logistics — The Journey into Robotics Begins

In 2007, Miyata joined Rakuten's logistics division and became Representative Director of Rakuten Logistics Inc. in 2010. He acquired France-based Alpha Direct Services and U.S.-based Webgistix, building a global logistics network. Starting in 2012, he began co-developing logistics robots with Hitachi. After prototyping, he announced "Racrew" in 2014. However, Miyata realized that true innovation couldn't happen within a large corporation.

PREHISTORY
2012
2012

The Kiva Systems Impact — Amazon Acquires for $775M

Amazon acquired Kiva Systems for approximately $775 million. This news shook the logistics industry and confirmed that logistics robotics would become the next generation of infrastructure. Miyata became convinced that Japan needed the same kind of transformation.

Founded — A Dream Begins with One Person

"Make your dreams a reality with your own hands" — inspired by these words from former Sony Chairman Norio Ohga, Hiroyuki Miyata left his position as Representative Director of Rakuten Logistics to establish GROUND Inc. The company was founded with a single vision: to create "Intelligent Logistics" by merging technology and logistics. Everything began with one person's determination.
