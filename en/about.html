Our Journey

History

2015

Company Founding

GROUND Inc. established in Tokyo with a mission to transform the logistics industry.

2016

AI Optimization Platform Development Launched

Research and development began on machine learning-powered logistics optimization solutions.

2018

GWES v1.0 Release

Launched GWES, our intelligent logistics system. Multiple logistics companies began adopting our platform.

2019

PEER Deployment Begins

Started commercial deployment of PEER, our autonomous mobile robot, driving automation in logistics operations.

2020

Major Partnership Expansion

Established strategic partnerships with leading logistics providers. Deployment exceeded 50 facilities.

2022

GPIM Methodology Established

Formalized GPIM (GROUND Process Improvement Methodology) and expanded consulting services.

2024

New Management Structure

Transitioned to new management framework for global expansion. Began operations in Asia-Pacific region.

2025

GWES v3.0 Launch

Released next-generation GWES v3.0 with dramatically improved AI prediction accuracy and user experience.
